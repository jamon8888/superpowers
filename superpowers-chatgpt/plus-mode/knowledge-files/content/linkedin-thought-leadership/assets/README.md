# Assets Directory

This directory contains YOUR personalized LinkedIn content assets. The more you populate these folders, the more tailored and effective the LinkedIn Thought Leadership skill becomes.

## Directory Structure

### `/audience-insights/`
Store analytics and insights about YOUR audience:
- Demographics and job titles
- Engagement patterns (what content performs best)
- Geographic distribution
- Industry breakdown

### `/case-studies/`
Document YOUR successful projects and client work:
- Real examples from your experience
- Quantifiable results and outcomes
- Lessons learned
- Reusable templates

### `/examples/`
Save YOUR high-performing LinkedIn posts:
- Posts that got exceptional engagement
- Different formats that worked well
- Successful hooks and CTAs
- Voice and tone examples

### `/frameworks/`
Create and store YOUR unique frameworks:
- Methodologies you've developed
- Mental models you use
- Process frameworks
- Strategic approaches

### `/templates/`
Build YOUR reusable post templates:
- Formats that work for you
- Story structures you use frequently
- Your go-to CTAs
- Post scaffolds for different topics

### `/voice-samples/`
Capture YOUR authentic voice:
- Examples of your natural writing
- Phrases you commonly use
- Your communication style
- Voice characteristics

## How to Use

1. **Start Small**: Begin with voice-samples and examples
2. **Build Gradually**: Add templates as you identify patterns
3. **Update Regularly**: Refresh with new successful content
4. **Be Specific**: The more specific and personal, the better

## Progressive Personalization

**Week 1-2**: Add 3-5 voice samples and your best posts
**Month 1**: Create 2-3 post templates that work for you
**Month 3**: Document your frameworks and case studies
**Month 6**: Full asset library with regular updates

The skill becomes exponentially more valuable as you build this library.
